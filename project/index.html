Computing Project

This module involves the development of a Computing Project and the
production of a Dissertation.

The learning objectives of the module are:

  • To systematically identify research problem(s) within a suitable research topic involving computing,
  • To carry out, structure, and present effectively a focused critical and analytical reading and synthesis of accessed information on the topic,
  • To articulate the professional, legal, ethical and social issues related to the chosen topic and follow relevant professional codes of conduct,
  • To evaluate critically the research conducted and present the resulting artefact as a form of a defence of the research.
