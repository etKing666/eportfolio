Secure Software
Development

The focus of this module is secure software development practices which also includes the review of the traditional and contemporary Software Development Life Cycle
(SDLC) models.

The learning objectives of the module are:

  • To be able to identify & manage security risks as part of a software development project,
  • To critically analyse development problems and determine appropriate methodologies, tools and techniques(including program design and development)to solve them,
  • To design, develop and adapt computer programs and to produce a solution that meets the design brief and critically evaluate solutions that are produced,
  • To systematically develop and implement the skills required to be effective member of a development team in a virtual professional environment, adopting real-life perspectives on team roles and organisation.
Source: Univeristy of Essex Online website (Accessed: 19 September 2022).
